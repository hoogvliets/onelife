Configuration Management

How It Works

Your widget configuration is automatically saved in your browser's local storage. You can export and import your configuration as a YAML file to:

  • Backup your widgets and settings
  • Transfer your configuration to another browser or device
  • Share your setup with others
  • Keep multiple configuration profiles

What Gets Saved

  • Widgets: All your widgets
  • Widget Content: Bookmark URLs, launchpad sites, note text, todo items
  • Settings: Theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Weather: Only locations (weather data is fetched fresh on import)
